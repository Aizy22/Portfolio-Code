About Me

I am a versatile and detail-oriented Web Developer with hands-on experience building full-featured systems using PHP and MySQL—most notably a tailor management system that handles everything from appointments to order tracking. While I’ve also explored Django through smaller projects like a to-do app, I’m continuing to deepen my understanding of its built-in tools and structure.


Beyond web development, I’ve delved into data analysis using Python libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, Seaborn, and NLTK to explore and visualize datasets, extract insights, and experiment with natural language processing.


Over time, I’ve become increasingly interested in integrating AI and automation into everyday workflows. I’ve built simple chatbots using Landbot and Dialogflow, and created automation pipelines with tools like Relevance AI, n8n, Airtable, and Make. These projects have helped me develop a strong foundation in combining AI, NLP, and no-code platforms to enhance product functionality.


I’m excited to keep growing in this space—blending solid backend development skills with emerging technologies to build smarter, more human-centered systems.
